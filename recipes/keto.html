Peanut butter chicken

This Peanut Butter Chicken is a low-carb variation of the traditional peanut butter chicken dish, designed to fit into a ketogenic (keto) diet. The goal of keto-friendly versions is to keep the dish rich in healthy fats and moderate in protein while minimizing the carbohydrate content.

Ingridients
  • Chicken
  • Peanut Butter
  • Coconut Milk
  • Soy Sauce or Tamari
  • Garlic and Ginger:
Steps
  1. Cook the Chicken: Start by cooking the chicken in a pan with a little olive oil, salt, and pepper until golden brown and cooked through. Set aside.
  2. Make the Peanut Sauce: In the same pan, combine peanut butter, coconut milk, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, lime juice, and spices. Simmer until the sauce thickens to your desired consistency.
  3. Combine: Once the sauce is ready, add the cooked chicken back into the pan, coat it in the sauce, and simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Serve: Serve your Keto Peanut Butter Chicken with a side of cauliflower rice, saut√©ed vegetables, or a simple salad.
